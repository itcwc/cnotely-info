Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use cnotely?

No. The web-converter on our homepage and basic download utilities are completely free and don't require credentials. Authentication is only needed if you wish to connect automated push pipelines to GitHub or Google Drive.

Where does my text go after conversion?

Nowhere. The parsing algorithm compiles text parameters purely inside local browser memories. Closing the tab immediately destroys active staging arrays unless manually exported.
