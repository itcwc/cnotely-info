Frequently Asked Questions

Why store notes on GitHub or Google Drive?

Storing notes on your own cloud ensures data sovereignty and avoids platform lock-in. Your knowledge stays with you forever as standard .md or .html files.

Is my data safe with cnotely?

cnotely is a UX Layer, not a storage provider. We use OAuth 2.0 for secure access and never store your note content in our own database.
